# Certified Implementation Specialist – Data Foundations (CMDB and CSDM) Beta Exam

*Updated November 2025*

## Introduction

The ServiceNow Data Foundations (CMDB and CSDM) Exam Specification document defines the purpose, audience, testing options, exam content coverage, test framework, and prerequisites for earning the Data Foundations (CMDB and CSDM) certification.

---

## Exam Purpose

The Certified Implementation Specialist – Data Foundations (CMDB and CSDM) (CIS-DF (CMDB and CSDM)) exam confirms you have the knowledge and skills to successfully design, implement, and operationalize a ServiceNow CMDB supporting the CSDM framework.

---

## Exam Audience

The ServiceNow Data Foundations (CMDB and CSDM) exam is available to ServiceNow customers, partners, employees, and others interested in implementing and operationalizing a ServiceNow CMDB supporting the CSDM framework.

### Intended Audiences *(this is not a comprehensive list)*

| Audience | Job Roles | Major Job Responsibilities |
|---|---|---|
| **Customer** | CMDB Administrator, CMDB Librarian/Data Steward, Configuration Manager, CI Class Owner, Platform Architect, Platform Owner, ServiceNow Developer, ServiceNow Technical Consultant | Administer and govern CMDB data; Implement processes for continuous improvement of the CMDB; Configure and review data provided on the CMDB Workspace, CMDB Data Foundations Dashboard, and reports to remediate issues and improve the overall health of the CMDB data; Perform regular audits and data validation to ensure CMDB data is accurate, complete, and up to date; Design, configure, and ensure the successful implementation of the CMDB |
| **Implementation Partner** | ServiceNow Technical Consultant | Design, configure, and ensure the successful implementation of the CMDB |

### Recommended Experience

- General familiarity with industry standards, terminology, and acronyms
- Certified System Administrator Certification
- 2+ years of experience with the CMDB
- Foundational knowledge of the CSDM model
- Experience populating data in the CMDB
- Remediation of compliance audit failure
- Managing duplicate CIs
- Addressing CI lifecycle processes (retiring or archiving stale CIs)
- Familiarity with SN products that use CMDB
- Familiarity with multi-source/CMDB 360

## Exam Structure

| | |
|---|---|
| **Exam Duration** | 90 minutes |
| **Number of Items** | 75 questions |

### Item Types

**Matching Item**
Each matching question presents two lists of related terms, concepts, or statements. Candidates must correctly pair items from one list with their corresponding matches in the other. There are 3 types:
- Each list contains the same number of items with a one-to-one match.
- One list contains more options than the other, requiring candidates to identify correct matches without using all options.
- Items in the second list can match to more than one item from the first list.

Each item in the first list has only one correct match. Partial credit is not awarded.

**Multiple Choice (single answer)**
At least three possible responses. Select the one correct response.

**Multiple Select (select all that apply)**
At least four possible responses. The question states how many responses to select. Select ALL correct responses. Partial credit is not provided.

**Scenario Based Item**
Presents a real-world situation or problem requiring analysis and application of knowledge. May be formatted as either multiple choice or multiple select.

## Exam Result

An exam result notification is immediately displayed as a **\*conditional pass or fail** after completing and submitting the exam.

An exam result report shows your result and the percentage of items in each section answered correctly. Results are preliminary and may be updated upon final review.

> *\*A conditional result means the exam can be audited and reviewed at any time, and the certification may be revoked if ServiceNow Test Security Policies are found to have been violated.*

### Understanding Your Results

Each question is worth one point. Your total score is compared to a predetermined cut score (not publicly shared, not always 70%) to determine pass or fail. Section percentages show domain performance but do not determine your overall result, should not be averaged, and do not indicate expertise.

### If You Pass

- You will earn the **Certified Implementation Specialist – Data Foundations (CMDB and CSDM)** certification and receive a **Credly digital badge**.
- To maintain your certification, complete annual maintenance exams (deltas) and pay the yearly **Certification Maintenance Program (CMP)** fee.

### If You Do Not Pass

- Review the exam blueprint and recommended training before retaking.
- The retake policy requires a **waiting period** between attempts and **payment of the exam fee** for each attempt.
- Refer to the Credentialing Program Guide for full retake details.

## Sample Questions

### Sample Question #1

The Configuration Management team wants to identify which CIs have been created from an asset but have not been discovered. The team creates a report on the target CI Class.

**How does the team identify these CIs in the report?**

- A. All CIs with a Discovery source value of `SNAssetManagement`
- B. All CIs with the Updated field older than a month
- C. All CIs where the Last Discovered field is empty
- D. All CIs with a Discovery source value of `ServiceNow`

**Answer: A**

---

### Sample Question #2

A State field is updated on an Asset record. Which fields are automatically synchronized on the associated CI record? *(choose two)*

- A. Operational status
- B. Install Status
- C. Hardware Status
- D. Asset tag

**Answer: B, C**
